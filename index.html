A Heritage of Excellence

Established in 1996, Haiyi has spent over thirty years facilitating the dialogue between discerning families and world-renowned educational institutions. Our history includes pivotal contributions to the sector, from the launch of the International Boarding Abroad Fair (iBAF) in 2005 to serving as a long-term strategic execution partner for global organizations such as TABS and Swiss Learning.
