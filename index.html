Haiyi Education Group is back after a post-pandemic hiatus — reorganized but continuing a legacy that dates to 1996. As the pioneer of China’s first international boarding school fairs in 2005 and organizer of more than a decade of events for TABS, Haiyi has long been recognized for its in-depth expertise in Swiss and Anglo educational systems.

Our team combines extensive experience in student placement for both secondary and university studies with institutional representation.
